Bits + Finance

We're trying to replace your bank, insurance company, broker, exchange, etc. with simple 1s and 0s. We at Variable Labs believe that code > middlemen. For the first time ever, Blockchain technology makes it possible to achieve this mission. Although, it is quite inaccessible. Our first course of action is to change that.

Dome

Banking, without the bank. Dome let's you Deposit, Withdraw, Lend, Borrow, Exchange and Transfer your money from anywhere at anytime in anyway you want.

Bracket

In simple words, Brackets are financial legos. They are modular services or products that anyone can add onto their lives.

TAO

TAO or The Autonomous Organisation is a fully blockchain-based entity that governs the development of Dome, Bracket and everything else we build. Holding X Tokens entail you membership in TAO.
